Chester Bennington

The Man Who Inspired Billions to Live

About

Chester Bennington is an american rock singer. He is best known for being the front man of alternative rock band Linkin Park and Dead By Sunrise. He is one of the top singers of his Genre.

Chester Bennington was born on March 20, 1976 in Phoenix, Arizona. He took interest in music at a young age, citing Depeche Mode and Stone Temple Pilots as early inspirations. His parents divorced when he was 11 years old and he started abusing Marijuana, Alcohol, Cocaine and LSD. Bennington first began singing with a band called Sean Dowdell and His Friends?. He then moved on to Grey Daze. He left Grey Daze in 1998. He later joined Mike Shinoda and his band Linkin Park. The band has hits such as Numb, Faint, Crawling and In The End.

Chester Bennington has one child, Jaime, from a previous relationship with Elka Brand. He also adopted his son, Isaiah. Bennington married his first wife, Samantha Marie Olit, on October 31, 1996. He then married Talinda Ann Bentley, a former Playboy model, with whom he has three children: Tyler Lee Bennington and twins Lilly and Lila (born 2011). The wedding ceremony was custom designed. They live in Gilbert, Arizona.

On July 20, 2017, Chester Bennington was found dead in his home. He had committed suicide by hanging. It was reported that he was very upset following the death of his friend Chris Cornell. Bennington committed suicide on what would have been Cornell's 53rd birthday.
